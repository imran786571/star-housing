ABOUT THE DIRECTORY,


PakRealEstate.com is the brainchild of our founder Mr Zahid Iqbal, who has worked for over 36 years in the Real Estate Industry and has made it his vision to promote and uplift the Real Estate Industry of Pakistan. The portal has its roots in the original print version of the Real Estate Directory for Defence and Clifton Real Estate Professionals that was started back in 1997. As the world of business evolves and moves more into the online space, we are now taking the directory online and expanding it significantly in order to facilitate the growth of the overall industry.
